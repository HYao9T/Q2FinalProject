About Soul Recipe

Discover the heart and soul of Filipino cuisine, where every dish tells a story of family, culture, and tradition. Whether you're craving the comforting warmth of adobo, the vibrant flavors of sinigang, or the sweet indulgence of halo-halo, you've come to the right place!At Savor Filipino, we celebrate the rich culinary heritage of the Philippines, offering authentic recipes, modern twists, and cooking tips to help you bring the taste of the islands to your table. Whether you're a seasoned home cook or a curious foodie eager to explore new flavors, our collection of recipes will guide you through every step of the way. Join us as we dive into the art of cooking, the joy of sharing meals, and the love that makes Filipino food truly special. Let's cook, connect, and savor the flavors of the Philippines together!

Sans Rival


A dessert cake made of layers of buttercream, meringue, and chopped cashews

Leche Flan


Leche Flan is a rich and creamy Filipino dessert, a crème caramel dessert made with caramelized sugar, egg yolks, and condensed milk, steamed to perfection.

Maja blanca

Maja Blanca is a creamy Filipino dessert made from coconut milk, cornstarch, and sugar, often topped with toasted coconut or corn kernels.
