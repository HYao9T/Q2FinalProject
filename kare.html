Kare-kare Recipe

    Ingredients
  • 1 kg oxtail or beef short ribs (cut into pieces)
  • 1 banana blossom (optional)
  • 1/2 cup peanut butter
  • 1/4 cup toasted rice flour (optional)
  • 1 onion (chopped)
  • 2 tomatoes (chopped)
  • 2 tbsp bagoong (fermented shrimp paste)
  • 1/4 cup cooking oil
  • 4 cups water
    Instructions
  1. Boil the oxtail or beef in water for 1-2 hours until tender.
  2. In a separate pan, saut√© onion and tomatoes in oil until soft. Add the peanut butter and toasted rice flour, stirring until smooth.
  3. Add the cooked meat and its broth into the peanut butter mixture. Stir well and simmer for 10 minutes.
  4. Season with salt or bagoong to taste.
  5. Add vegetables like banana blossom, string beans, and eggplant, and cook for another 5-10 minutes until tender.
  6. Serve with bagoong on the side.
